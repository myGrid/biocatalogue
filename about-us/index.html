The ServiceCatalographer is brought to you by the myGrid team. You can contact us at support@mygrid.org.uk.

The team produces a suite of tools designed to “help e-Scientists get on with science and get on with scientists”, including:

  • Taverna – for design, editing and execution of workflows
  • myExperiment – for sharing of workflows and related data
  • Sysmo-DB – for finding, sharing and exchanging data, models and processes in systems biology
  • RightField – for adding ontology term selection to Excel spreadsheets
  • WF4Ever – for preservation of scientific workflows
  • MethodBox – for browsing datasets and sharing the knowledge
